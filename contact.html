Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get started?

You can get started in minutes! Simply sign up for a free account and start building your first dashboard. No credit card required.

What devices are supported?

We support all major IoT platforms including Arduino, ESP32, Raspberry Pi, and industrial PLCs. Check our hardware compatibility page for the full list.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! We offer a free tier that includes up to 10 devices and basic features. You can upgrade anytime to unlock advanced features.

Do you offer enterprise support?

Absolutely! Our enterprise plans include dedicated support, custom integrations, and SLA guarantees. Contact our sales team for more information.
